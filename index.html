About

The Project

Friends, Space Romans, countrymen: Broadside is a volunteer-built sci-fi FPS-Z almost a year into development. The team is building a modern arena shooter around movement, vehicles, base building, and long sightlines for a single action-packed sci-fi experience.

The project is being built in Unreal Engine 5.4 by a core team of designers, programmers, audio engineers, mappers, technical artists, dev-ops, and VFX specialists. The missing piece is art: concept work, 3D assets, textures, and UI.

Volunteer projectGit-based workflowBuilt in UE 5.4Profit shared with members

Volunteer-built world

The visual direction prioritizes tactile sci-fi surfaces, readable silhouettes, and production art that can move directly into the game.

Gameplay

Core Experience

Fast Movement

Momentum-heavy traversal with a focus on speed, air control, and clean player readability.

Indoor Combat

Tight tactical spaces built for aggressive peeks, pressure, and close-quarters fights.

Open Terrain

Wide spaces that reward positioning, route choice, and aggressive map movement.

Vehicles

High-speed traversal and mounted firepower designed to keep the field moving.

Base Building

Defensive structures and fortified nodes that support team play and control.
